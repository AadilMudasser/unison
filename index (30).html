01 — About

The room does the talking. We just cut well.

Unison was built for men who don't want a performance — they want a result.

We stripped the shop back to its shell: exposed concrete, low light, no music competing for attention. What's left is the work itself — a straight parting, a clean fade line, a blade that's been stropped properly.

Every barber at Unison trains on the same standard before they're allowed near a guest's chair: measured proportion, not guesswork. It's the difference between a haircut and a cut that holds its shape for three weeks.
